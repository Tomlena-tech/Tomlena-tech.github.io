About
"As a father, I just wanted to know she was safe — without making her feel watched."

There's a feeling every parent knows — that quiet unease when you don't know where your child is. Not panic. Just a low hum that doesn't go away until they walk through the door.

I didn't want surveillance. I wanted something discreet — a safety net that respects her independence while giving me peace of mind. WIMC was built from that need.

Built solo across 5 sprints, 85 hours, 73 commits — from scratch.

Portfolio Project for Holberton School — Full-Stack & AI Development, Bordeaux 2025.
