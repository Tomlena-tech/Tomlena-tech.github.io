The Story
"There's a quiet unease every parent knows — the moment you don't know where your child is."

As a father, I know that feeling well. Not panic — just a low, persistent hum at the back of your mind until she walks through the door, or sends a message, or you spot her on the map.

I didn't want surveillance. I didn't want an app that makes my daughter feel watched every minute of her life. I wanted something discreet — a quiet safety net that respects her independence while giving me peace of mind.

WIMC was built from that need. A real GPS tracking app, with safe zones, AI integration, and a clean mobile experience — built from scratch across 5 sprints in 85 hours.

Portfolio Project for Holberton School — Full-Stack & AI Development, Bordeaux 2025.
